Privacy Policy.

1.Introduction

Welcome to "My Event," a website dedicated to event management, notification, and planning services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other contact information when you register and use our services.

Event Information: We collect information about the events you create and manage on our platform, including event details, expenditure, attendance lists, and payment details.

Pledges: When managing pledges for your event, we may collect information related to pledge amounts, payment schedules, and the identity of pledge donors.

Electronic Invitations: If you use our service to send electronic invitations, we collect recipient email addresses, RSVP responses, and event-related preferences.

Log Data: We collect information on how you use our website, including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and the time and date of your interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • Event Management: To facilitate the planning, organization, and management of events, including expenditure tracking, attendance lists, and payment processing.
  • Communication: To send event notifications, updates, and electronic invitations to attendees.
  • Pledge Management: To keep track of pledges and manage payment schedules.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze user behavior, troubleshoot issues, and enhance the quality of our services.
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, or illegal activities.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers, such as payment processors and email service providers, only to the extent necessary to provide our services.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

6. Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your personal information through your account settings. If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us at [Contact Email].

7. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience. You can adjust your browser settings to manage or disable cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
